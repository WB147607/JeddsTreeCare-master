Emrald Ash-Bore

Fun facts

1.The Emerald Ash Borer (EAB) is actually native to Asia including China, Korea, and Japan

2.With the help of dendrochronological reconstruction (a method of scientific dating based on the analysis of tree rings), scientists have traced EAB destruction back to the early 1990’s in Michigan.

3.An EAB beetle’s lifespan ranges from 1-2 years in North America.

4.The EAB beetle kills ash trees by destroying the water and nutrient conducting tissues under the tree’s bark. A tree will die within two years of signs of the EAB beetle.

5.The Emerald Ash Borer is now recognized as the most harmful pest in North America
